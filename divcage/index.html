Welcome to Scribbler

Step 1:

Grab something green. Anything will do, but brighter greens and good lighting work best.

Step 2:

Draw

In order to begin drawing, your browser will ask for access to your webcam. Click 'Allow', then 'Draw' and you're ready to go.

Step 3:

Talk

Choose colors manually or with your voice. Click 'Talk', allow use of your microphone, and say the name of the color you want to paint with.
